Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant city

Top three activities to do at Pisa

Visit the Leaning Tower of Pisa

Iconic tilted tower; climb for panoramic views and capture unforgettable photos of this architectural wonder.

Explore Piazza dei Miracoli

Stunning UNESCO site with the Leaning Tower, Cathedral, Baptistery, and Camposanto showcasing medieval art and architecture.

Stroll Along the Arno River

Scenic riverside strolls and vibrant square with cafes, markets, and authentic local charm.
